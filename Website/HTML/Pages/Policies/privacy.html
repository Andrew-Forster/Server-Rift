1. Information We Collect

At Server Rift, we collect the following types of information:

Preferences: Your settings and preferences on our platform.

Usernames: Your chosen username from Discord.

Authorization Tokens: Secure tokens used for authentication and authorization within our platform.

Discord Server Membership and Roles: Information about the Discord servers you are a member of and your roles within those servers. This information is essential for providing you with a tailored experience on Server Rift.

2. Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect about you in the following ways:

To Enhance Your Experience: We use your preferences and roles to tailor your experience on Server Rift, making it more enjoyable and relevant to you.

To Improve Server Rift: We analyze collected information to understand how users interact with our platform and identify areas for improvement.

For Security Purposes: Your authorization tokens are used to securely verify your identity and protect your account from unauthorized access. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Age Restriction

Server Rift is intended for use by individuals who are at least 18 years old. Users under the age of 18 are welcome to use Server Rift only if they have obtained explicit parental consent. By using Server Rift, you represent that you meet this age requirement or have the required consent.

4. Data Protection

We take the security of your personal information seriously and employ technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Despite our efforts, no security measures are completely impervious, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes and post the updated policy on our platform.
